Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a vacate clean take in Perth?

Most properties take between 4 and 8 hours depending on size and condition. A 2-bedroom apartment typically takes around 4–5 hours; a 4-bedroom house can take 7–9 hours. We always allow enough time to do the job properly.

Is carpet steam cleaning included in the vacate clean?

Vacuuming is always included. Professional steam cleaning is an add-on service and is required by many Perth landlords under their lease agreements. We offer combined bookings at a discounted rate.

Do I need to be home during the clean?

No. Most clients hand over a key or code and we let ourselves in. We'll send you photos and an update when we're finished, and you can check the property before your final inspection.

What does the bond-back guarantee actually mean?

If your property manager identifies a cleaning issue from our work within 72 hours of the clean, we return and fix it at absolutely no cost. Our record speaks for itself — the vast majority of clients receive their full bond back first time.

Do you clean in all Perth suburbs?

Yes. We service the full metropolitan area including the northern and southern suburbs, hills, and inner-city. There are no travel surcharges for most Perth postcodes.

How much does a vacate clean cost in Perth?

Prices vary based on property size, condition, and any add-ons. We provide upfront, obligation-free quotes — visit vacatecleaningperth.net to get yours instantly.
